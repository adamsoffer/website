Over the past 18 months, the Livepeer ecosystem has experienced significant momentum. The introduction of an onchain treasury for funding public goods through community governance, combined with community alignment around realtime AI video infrastructure, has driven increasing network fees and usage. The newly launched Livepeer Foundation now stewards the 10+ ecosystem entities serving as core project contributors.

Livepeer Inc is now shifting focus toward executing a high-conviction go-to-market motion to dramatically grow demand on the Livepeer network. The company remains committed to the realtime AI video vision outlined in Livepeer Cascade and is concentrating exclusively on productization to achieve product-market fit.

## Product Market Fit for Realtime AI Video

The company's top priority involves three core initiatives:

1. **Core network development** ensuring Livepeer can execute realtime AI video workflows
2. **Developer APIs** extending Livepeer Studio capabilities for application integration
3. **Community cultivation** establishing a hub for researchers, builders, and creators in realtime AI video

## What Livepeer Inc Is NOT Focusing On

The company deliberately narrows its scope, recognizing that a single company is more likely to succeed by focusing on one opportunity at a time.

- **Transcoding alone** — technically and economically viable but lacks market adoption without enterprise-grade support
- **End-to-end streaming** — Livepeer Studio continues supporting existing enterprise customers, but the company won't actively compete for growth in this segment
- **Web3 social and decentralized streaming** — better served by projects like Streamplace and other SPEs aligned with the Foundation
- **Additional realtime AI applications** — visual avatars for AI agents and video analysis fall within the broader mission but represent opportunities for other ecosystem builders

## Foundation and Inc: A Simplified Framework

- **Livepeer Foundation** stewards community, project marketing, and public goods funding
- **Livepeer Inc** drives network demand through realtime AI products, go-to-market services, and community building

Abundant opportunities exist for ecosystem builders to address segments Livepeer Inc isn't pursuing.
